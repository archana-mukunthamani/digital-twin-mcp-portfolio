## 📈 Posting Best Practices

### Timing
- **Best times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12-2 PM
- **Avoid**: Late nights, weekends, holidays

### Hashtags
Use **5-10 relevant hashtags**:
- 2-3 broad (#AI, #MachineLearning)
- 3-4 specific (#RAG, #NextJS, #VectorDatabase)
- 2-3 career-focused (#Portfolio, #Internship)

### Length
- **Optimal**: 150-300 words (above template is ~200)
- Shorter = more engagement
- Use line breaks for readability

### Call to Action
Always include a CTA:
- "Check out the code"
- "Try the live demo"
- "Let me know your thoughts"
- "Open to collaboration!"
